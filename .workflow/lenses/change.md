# Lens: Change

Use this lens when the work is large enough to deserve a tracked change workspace.

## Add Only When Needed

- Work spans multiple tasks or days.
- Public interfaces, long-term rules, or architecture may change.
- Completion needs explicit evidence and archive notes.

## Outputs

- `.docs/changes/{change_id}/brief.md`
- `.docs/changes/{change_id}/plan.md`
- `.docs/changes/{change_id}/evidence.md`
- `.docs/changes/{change_id}/archive.md`
